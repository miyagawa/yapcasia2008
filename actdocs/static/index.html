YAPC::Asia 2008

YAPC::Asia 2008 will happen May 15-16, 2008 in Tokyo, JAPAN.

The venue will be Tokyo Institute of Technology: very accessible from central Tokyo within 30 minutes.

For the latest updates and communication, we have news feed, Twitter stream and Facebook event page. You don't need to double register on this site and Facebook, but it'll help people know each other more!

"YAPC" stands for "Yet Another Perl Conference" and is a series of low-cost, grassroot conferences organized by local Perl user groups. YAPC::Asia 2008 is the 3rd YAPC in Tokyo. Check out our site 2006 and 2007 for what happend in previous years.
